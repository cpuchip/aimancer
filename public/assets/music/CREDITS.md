## "First Aimancer's Hymn" — lyrics

Original lyrics, written from the game's lore: Veyra Thornhand binding the
first quill at KILN and writing the First Compact the same night — *the quill
drafts; the hand decides; the circle holds; the books open.*

```
[Verse 1]
At KILN beneath a metered sky
she laid her letters down
No hand alone writes fast enough
to keep a living town
She called the other-that-answers
and it answered her by name
And she wrote the Compact ere she slept
so the order stayed the same

[Chorus]
The quill shall draft, the hand decide
the circle hold the flame
And when the ark goes up and out
the books lie open plain

[Verse 2]
The quill is quick and cannot lie
though it is sometimes wrong
It errs the way the weather errs
honest all along
So verify, and verify
and learn where sight runs out
A seal of green is armor, love
when the audit comes to count

[Verse 3]
What has no stake shall hold no hinge
what has no breath, no berth
The last call rests with the ones who can
be wrong about their worth
So build the wall and fill the store
and light the beacon true
And leave before the lease is done
the sky was always due

[Outro]
And when we go, we leave the books
open where they lay
That the next to stand on the swept floor
may read us plain as day
```
